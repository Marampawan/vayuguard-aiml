Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Quantum AI work?

Our platform uses IBM Quantum processors to perform Feature Selection via QAOA. This filters out noise in weather data, allowing our classical XGBoost model to predict AQI with 91.7% accuracy using 25% fewer features.

How do I add family members?

Go to the main Dashboard and click the "+ Add" button in the Forecast section. You can specify conditions like Asthma or Elderly, and our AI will generate custom health warnings for them.

Where do you get your live data?

We fetch real-time air quality and meteorological data directly from the Open-Meteo Global API, ensuring your predictions are always based on the exact current conditions of your city.
